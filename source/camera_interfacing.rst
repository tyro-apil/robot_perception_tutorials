Camera Interfacing
=====================

Cameras are the most widely used sensor for robot perception. We can find many types of cameras in the market like 
RGB camera, depth camera, thermal camera, Time-of-Flight (ToF) camera, etc. But, RGB and depth cameras are the most popular.  
And, we will be learning how to interface and get data from them.
